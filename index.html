About Me

My name is Adam Bitz. I was born in Fargo North Dakota, but I spent the first eight years of my life living in Hillsboro. In the summer before second grade, I moved to Mandan, where I remained through my high school graduation. Throughout middle and high school, I remained active in many school clubs such as science club, rubiks cube club, and Latin club. I also competed for a variety of teams for my school. I played football through my freshman year of high school, competed on the academic olympics team in my sophomore and senior years, and played on multiple esports teams my junior and senior years.

Career

I graduated from Mandan High School in spring of 2021 and spent two years at Bismarck State College majoring in CyberSecurity. During that time, I began to realize that I enjoyed programming much more than normal CyberSecurity operations. I took an extra computer science course during the spring of my sophomore year, and after getting my Associate's degree, I transferred to NDSU and changed my major to Computer Science. This is my second semester at NDSU, and I'm hoping to land a solid internship over the summer in software development. After I graduate, I'm hoping to work as a software developer either in person or remotely in North Dakota.
